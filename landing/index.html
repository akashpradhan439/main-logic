Features

Why Lokaal Is Different

Not a dating app. Not a social network. A meeting app.

Spatial Awareness

Lokaal knows when you're near someone you'd click with — shared interests, similar vibes, same neighborhood.

Fair Meetups

Every suggestion picks a place that's convenient for both people. No one-sided "meet me on my side of town."

Private by Design

Your exact location is never exposed. Lokaal only knows you're "somewhere in this area" — never precisely where.

12+ Languages

Because real life happens everywhere. Lokaal speaks your language, wherever you are.

Works in Background

No need to keep the app open. Lokaal quietly works behind the scenes and only alerts you when something good happens.

Real Connections Only

Only suggests people you've actually encountered in the real world. No random strangers from the internet.

Process

How It Works

Three simple steps from encounter to friendship

01

Lokaal detects nearby people

As you go about your day, Lokaal quietly notices when you're in the same area as someone you'd get along with.

02

It suggests a specific place

Not a vague "you should hang out." A real café, a real park, a real restaurant — picked as a fair midpoint between both of you.

03

You both get a notification

"Hey, you and Sarah both love indie music — and there's a vinyl café halfway between you. This Saturday at 3pm?" Done.
